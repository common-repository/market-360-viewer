Market 360 viewer is a simplest way to put 360 products presentations on your Wordpress site.

== Description ==

Embed 360 product views created and downloaded from [Market360.co](http://market360.co) web application into your WordPress posts and pages. Thousands of 360 product packshots ready to use.

Market360 product viewer is a free WordPress plugin that can be used to effortlessly embed readymade rotary views of objects. Visit our site to see demo and download thousands 360 product views.
Features: • Quick and easy integration
• Access to thousands of readymade 360 product views.
• HTML5 compatible – works on iPads, iPhones, Android devices and popular desktop browsers
• 360 product view can be combined with additional photos and graphics - it is a complete product presentation
• Variety of settings for playback to control rotation direction and speed, zoom level, fullscreen enable, etc.
• Gradual, deep zoom and full screen option.
• Technical support
• Host 360 product views on your server – no addition fees

How to create 360 object view.
Go to [Market360.co](http://market360.co) and use an easy online creator of 360 product view. Download your 360 presentation on local machine and use Market360 product viewer plugin to embed it into your WordPress posts or pages.
You can use readymade 360 product views which are constantly updated in Market360.co

== Installation ==

This section describes how to install the plugin and get it working.

e.g.

1. Upload the entire `market360-viewer` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
